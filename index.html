FAQs

What materials are used in your jewelry and watches?

Our jewelry and watches are crafted from high-quality materials, including 18K gold, sterling silver, stainless steel, and genuine gemstones. Each piece is designed for durability and elegance.

Do you offer warranties on your products?

Yes! We offer a one-year warranty on all watches and a six-month warranty on jewelry. This covers manufacturing defects but not damage caused by accidents or improper use.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your order in the "My Orders" section of your account.
